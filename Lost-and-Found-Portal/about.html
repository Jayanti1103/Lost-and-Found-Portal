Our Story

We believe that what is lost can, and should, be found. This portal was born from the simple idea of replacing the dusty, disorganized cardboard box in the corner of an office with a smart, simple, and efficient digital solution.

Losing an item is stressful. Searching for it shouldn't be. Our mission is to use technology to connect finders and losers instantly, fostering a community of honesty and helping return valuable and sentimental items to their rightful owners.

What This Portal Does

Report Instantly

Lost or found something? File a report in under 60 seconds. Add details, a location, and a photo to make it easily identifiable.

Browse & Search

Filter through all reported items. Our searchable database lets you look for your item by keyword, date, or location.

Claim Securely

Found your item? Submit a claim with proof of ownership. This stops fraud and ensures items go to the right person.
